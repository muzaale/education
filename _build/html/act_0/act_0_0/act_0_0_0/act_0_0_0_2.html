AI as a tool to personalize experiences for students, patients, and family members

Chatbots are a great way to personalize experiences for students, patients, and family members. In this video, we'll explore how to use the ChatGPT model to create a chatbot that can be used to help students learn, patients get better care, and family members stay connected.

AI as a Tool to Personalize Experiences for Students, Patients, and Family Members#

Authors#

  1. Educator: Chief of UNICEF Liberia

  2. Academic and Clinical Researcher: Department of Surgery, American Medical School

  3. Elder Statesman: Retired Academic and Civil Servant, PhD from UC Berkeley School of Social Welfare

Abstract#

The digital revolution is changing the way we educate, provide healthcare, and connect with our families. In resource-limited settings, the challenge is to leverage technological advancements in a way that is accessible and sustainable. This essay delves into the use of AI-driven chatbots, particularly ChatGPT, as a medium to personalize educational content, healthcare interventions, and familial connections.

Introduction#

With increasing global connectivity and technological advancements, there’s a need to adapt and personalize services in education, healthcare, and social work. We have before us the untapped potential of AI-driven platforms like ChatGPT to revolutionize these sectors.

Education: Bridging the Gap in Resource-Limited Settings#

In nations where student-to-teacher ratios are incredibly high, the traditional classroom model is strained. ChatGPT can serve as a supplementary educational tool. By tailoring responses based on the student’s existing knowledge and learning style, ChatGPT can offer a personalized learning experience, filling in the gaps where human resources are limited.

The Liberia Case Study#

In Liberia, UNICEF has piloted a program that utilizes ChatGPT in rural schools. The bot assists in subjects where teacher expertise is scarce, contributing to better educational outcomes.

Healthcare: A Step Towards Personalized Medicine#

In the realm of clinical research, particularly in surgery, the focus is turning towards predictive modeling and risk assessment. Web apps and online risk calculators can be augmented with ChatGPT to provide real-time, individualized health assessments.

The Maryland Experience#

In Maryland, where clinical research in surgery is burgeoning, ChatGPT assists patients in navigating through the labyrinthine corridors of healthcare options, guiding them based on their medical history and risk factors.

Indigenizing Social Work: A Memoir in Motion#

For a decade, the dream of indigenizing social work has been conceptual but not fully realized. ChatGPT can serve as an archivist and collaborator. Conversations with the elder generation can be transformed into a digital memoir, a living document that evolves and captures indigenous knowledge and practice in social work.

The Ugandan Narrative#

Our father, with his decades of experience in academic and civil service, has been working on a magnum opus about indigenizing social work in Uganda. Through AI, we aim to bring his work to fruition by personalizing it to Ugandan society.

Conclusions#

We stand at a crossroads where technology can make personalization in education, healthcare, and social work not just a possibility but a reality. By utilizing AI-driven tools like ChatGPT, we can provide scalable solutions in resource-limited settings, enhancing education, healthcare, and preserving indigenous wisdom.

Future Directions#

  1. Expand the ChatGPT education pilot to more schools in Liberia.

  2. Incorporate real-time analytics in the healthcare predictive models in Maryland.

  3. Create a digital archive that includes our father’s work, rendering it accessible for future generations.

Thus, we find ourselves in an era where the lines between sectors are blurring. Personalization through AI, as we have shown, has the potential to revolutionize how we learn, how we heal, and how we connect with our roots. Technology is the pen with which we write the next chapter in each of these narratives, a chapter that is personal, inclusive, and accessible.
